## Security Policy

AquaCode recognizes the importance of security in its systems and services. We appreciate responsible vulnerability reporting and are committed to handling it with seriousness, confidentiality, and promptness.

Security reports must be submitted exclusively to:

**security@aquacode.ai**

---


## Scope Only Includes

- Official repositories under `github.com/aquacodeai/*`.
- Official apps, services, and APIs directly managed by AquaCode.

---


## Required Report Content

To support proper assessment and resolve, the report must include:

- Clear description of the vulnerability.
- Affected component, service, or repository.
- Reproducible steps (requests, or payloads used).
- Relevant technical evidence (such as HTTP responses or logs, excluding personal or user data).
- Date and time (UTC) when the test was carried out.
- Contact information and availability for follow-up.

---


## Timelines and Commitments

AquaCode commits to:

- **Confirm receipt of the report within a maximum of 2 days.**
- **Perform initial triage and provide a preliminary estimate within 5 days after confirmation.**
- Maintain periodic communication with the researcher until the case is resolved.
